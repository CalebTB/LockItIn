## ğŸ¯ Key Principles

**1. Fair Value Exchange**

- Free users get real value, not a crippled experience
- Premium users get genuine upgrades, not unlocked basics
- Price reflects value provided ($5/mo is fair)

**2. Transparent & Honest**

- No dark patterns or manipulation
- Clear what's free vs paid
- Easy to cancel, no tricks
- Respect user intelligence

**3. Privacy Always First**

- Never sell data, ever
- No tracking for ad purposes
- Premium features respect privacy too
- Monetize features, not user data

**4. Indie Developer Friendly**

- Reasonable pricing users will actually pay
- Sustainable revenue at modest scale
- Not trying to be a billion-dollar company
- Building a great product people love
