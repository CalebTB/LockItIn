# FLOW 6: VIEWING GROUP CALENDARS (AVAILABILITY)

```markdown
Groups Tab
  ↓
List of your friend groups:
  ├─ "College Friends" (8 members)
  ├─ "Roommates" (4 members)
  └─ "Basketball Crew" (6 members)
  ↓
Tap on "College Friends"
  ↓
Group Detail View
  ├─ Member list (with avatars)
  ├─ "View Group Calendar" button ⭐
  ├─ Upcoming group events
  └─ Past events
  ↓
Tap "View Group Calendar"
  ↓
Group Calendar View (overlay mode)
  ├─ Shows YOUR full calendar
  ├─ Shows FRIENDS' availability as colored blocks:
      ├─ Green = Everyone free
      ├─ Yellow = Most people free
      ├─ Red = Most people busy
      ├─ Gray = No data (privacy = private)
  └─ Can tap on a time block to see:
      └─ "Available: Sarah, Mike, Jordan"
      └─ "Busy: Alex (Busy), Chris (Private event)"
  ↓
Tap "Propose Event Here" on a green block
  ↓
Starts Flow 4 with pre-filled time
```
